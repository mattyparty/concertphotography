KISS

2.19.2020 @ Intrust Bank Arena

End of the Road Tour

My first memory of kiss comes from a faded picture from the 70s of my uncle and his high school cover band tucked away in my grandmothers basement. The costumes although hand made were extremely well done. I’m sure I looked at that picture in fascination over 50 times as a kid. I tell all people who are going to a kiss show get ready for some of the greatest showmanship you have ever seen. A kiss concert is a grand experience that all live music lovers should experience. You will see breathing fire and and people flying across the arena….. what more can you ask for!
